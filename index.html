Toast

This recipe was inspired by my great great great grandmother Elsie. She would make this for me every morning before school to help me develop a sudden and severe gluten intolerance that would later ruin my guts biodiversity. It was my fondest memory growing up but due to unforeseen circumstances I was never able to get my hands on the family recipe... Until now! I spent hours upon hours experimenting with different kinds of bread and butter combinations. After many restless nights I have finally come up an exact replica recipe! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  1. Bread, preferably made with calcium propionate, sodium stearoly-2-lactylate and sorbic acid.
  2. Butter, explicitly a highly processed vegetable oil.
  3. Toaster, any brand toaster will suffice.
  4. A will to live.

Steps

  1. Carefully place a slice of your favorite bread into the toaster.
  2. Turn toastiness dial/knob to your desired level.
  3. Press lever down on toaster to start the cooking process.
  4. Wait... wait... wait some more... POP... toast is done!
  5. Remove toast from toaster using only *conductive utensils*.
  6. Lather up your toast with copious amounts of butter.
  7. Eat and enjoy!
